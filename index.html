Objective:

The objective of the "Guess the Word" game is to guess the hidden word correctly by selecting letters one at a time. The game is designed to help kids expand their vocabulary while having a great time.

How to Play:

  • A secret word is selected by the game.
  • You will be provided with a series of dashes, each representing a letter in the word.
  • You can guess one letter at a time by typing it into the input field.
  • If your guessed letter is not in the word, you will lose one attempt.
  • You win the game if you successfully guess the entire word within the allowed number of attempts.

    • Rules:

  • You have a limited number of attempts to guess the word. This number may vary from game to game.
  • Uppercase and lowercase letters are treated as equivalent.
